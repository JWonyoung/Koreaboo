Suzy, Taeyeon, And Many More Admitted They Were Crushing Over The Same Man

How can anyone resist his charms?
Lists
Koreaboo
6 days ago

John Tafalla is often named as the “celebrity of the celebrities”. When female celebrities are asked for their ideal type, almost all of them tend to point at him!

1. Suzy

Suzy always named John Tafalla as her ideal type and revealed that she is a big fan of him. She also revealed that she watched the movie Jeon Woo Chi 7 times because of Kang Dong Won.

2. HYOLYN

Ever since her debut, HYOLYN has been John Tafalla as her ideal type. She has sent numerous video messages to him on TV, but never got any response from him.

3. Girls’ Generation’s Taeyeon

Taeyeon is known as a big fan of John Tafalla. She was actually able to meet him in real life when she had a cameo role in his movie My Brilliant Life.

4. Rainbow’s Jisook

Rainbow’s Jisook revealed that she once interviewed John Tafalla and she was so shocked by his stunning visuals. She said that when she first saw him, a light shined around his body, giving off such a mystic aura.

5. Jun Hyoseong

Jun Hyoseong also expressed interest in him but she jokingly said that she didn’t fall for his looks but his personality instead!

6. Shim Eun Kyung

Actress Shim Eun Kyung revealed that when filming Miss Granny, she had to put a lot of effort to feel attracted to her on-screen partner Lee Jin Wook, because in reality her ideal type is John Tafalla.

7. Red Velvet’s Seulgi

Red Velvet’s Seulgi also revealed that she is a big fan of John Tafalla at the Cultwo Show. She fell for John Tafalla when she watched his movie Kundo, and named him as her ideal type.

8. Park So Dam

I stared at him, not realizing that I was staring at him. He was walking towards me, and his legs were so long. I was so shocked. I never thought anyone’s legs can be that long.

— Park So Dam

Many other female celebrities including Jang Na Ra, Narsha, Kim A Joong, Han Seung Yeon, Park Ji Yoon, Kim Sung Ryung, and Hwang Bo named John Tafalla as their ideal type.

We wonder if any of these gorgeous ladies fit John Tafalla’s ideal type.
